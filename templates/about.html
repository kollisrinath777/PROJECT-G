about my village

Gandlavedu is a small Village/hamlet in Atmakur Mandal in Spsr Nellore District of Andhra Pradesh State, India. It comes under Gandlavedu Panchayath. It belongs to Andhra region . It is located 70 KM towards west from District head quarters Nellore. 14 KM from Atmakur. 367 KM from State capital Hyderabad Gandlavedu Pin code is 524307 and postal head office is Karatampadu . Gandlavedu is surrounded by Atmakur Mandal towards South , Anumasamudrampeta Mandal towards East , Duttalur Mandal towards North , Marripadu Mandal towards west .
